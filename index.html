About Us

Our Story

We started Ixolink because we were tired of seeing great teams waste time on bad data. Here’s how we’re changing that.

Built on Precision and Verified Data

Ixolink began with a simple observation: strong sales teams were being slowed down by unreliable data. Campaigns weren’t failing because of effort; they were failing because the foundation was flawed. We set out to fix that at the source.

Sales and marketing teams were using old, incomplete, or unconfirmed information, we saw. Bad data made campaigns weaker, less trustworthy and made people make choices based on guesses.

So we built a verification-based method that focuses on quality that can be seen and measured. We continually improving our methodology to help businesses contact the right people, improve their data infrastructure, and make choices based on facts instead of guesses.
